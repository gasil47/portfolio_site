Soda Frenzy

A fun and addictive soda-serving game designed for mobile devices

Overview

Soda Frenzy lets players pour the perfect soda cup under time pressure while avoiding spills and mistakes. It features intuitive controls, increasing challenge levels, and fun animations. I worked on player controls, customer interaction logic, and polishing the juice and soda particle effects.

Key Features

  • One-touch control mechanics
  • Progressive difficulty curve
  • Visual juice and foam effects
  • Character and environment animations
  • Performance optimization for Android

Tools & Technologies
