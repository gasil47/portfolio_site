Tangled Heist

A strategic line-drawing game with stealth mechanics and puzzle-solving

Overview

Tangled Heist challenges players to guide a group of thieves through secure paths while avoiding alarms and guards. I designed line-drawing mechanics, AI movement logic, and helped fine-tune the path collision detection systems.

Key Features

  • Drag-and-plan movement mechanics
  • AI enemies and guard patterns
  • Dynamic collision and alert zones
  • Multiple mission levels with increasing difficulty

Tools & Technologies
