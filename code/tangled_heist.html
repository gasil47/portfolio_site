Tangled Heist

A strategic line-drawing game with stealth mechanics and puzzle-solving

Overview

Tangled Heist challenges players to help a group of thieves tangled with ropes. I worked on all the part of the game, the grid logic, rope mechanics, and helped fine-tune visuals.

Key Features

  • Grid based rope Untangle
  • Timer based gameplay
  • Dynamic collision and alert zones
  • Multiple mission levels with increasing difficulty
