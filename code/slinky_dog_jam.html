Slinky Dog Jam

A fun arcade-style game with a stretchy dog and tricky levels

Overview

Slinky Dog Jam is a fast-paced arcade game where players control a slinky dog navigating platforms, avoiding traps, and reaching the goal with flexible physics-based movement. I focused on slinky stretching mechanics, obstacle logic, and level progression.

Key Features

  • Physics-based stretchy movement
  • Platform challenges and obstacles
  • Unique cartoon-style visuals
  • Progressive level unlocking

Tools & Technologies

  • Unity (C#)
