VR Flight Simulator

Immersive aerial experience built for Meta Quest 2

Overview

VR Flight Simulator is an immersive 3D experience developed specifically for Meta Quest 2. Players take control of a virtual aircraft in a fully interactive environment. Designed for intuitive VR controls and smooth physics, the game simulates a real flight cockpit and terrain navigation.

Key Features

  • Full VR cockpit with interactable controls
  • Realistic flight physics and environments
  • Compatible with Meta Quest 2 and VR controllers
  • Haptic feedback and immersive audio
  • Training missions and free-fly mode
